Strategy Map

This is our strategy map that shows the capabilities that IITB and ESDC need to continuously improve as well as the evolution towards the government as a service (OneGC) vision included in the Digital Operations Strategic Plan: 2018-2022 and the Policy on Service and Digital.

The links current lead to the DevOps Research and Assessment (DORA) DevOps Capabilities. Links will be updated to point to specific strategies and theories of change (how will changes occur in IT teams) for each capability.
